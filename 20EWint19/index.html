Midterms and Final

  • For the midterms and final there will be assigned seats. You need to bring a writing utensil to the midterms and final. There will be room for you to write your answers on the test. You do not need to bring a blue book. You are not allowed to use any electronic aids (calculator, cell phone, etc.). You must bring a photo id (preferably your stuent id) to the midterms and final. You are allowed one hand written 8.5in x 11in sheet of paper of notes. You are allowe to write on both sides.
